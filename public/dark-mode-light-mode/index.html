Dark mode, light mode, balcony

Everybody says dark mode is good. Or around 95% of people. But they say it's not based on clean data because the default setting was dark.

Everybody says light mode is good. Or at least some punk bloggers and scientists. But they say we should not trust scientists anymore (rightfully so?).

I don't know if dark mode is good or bad (it's probably both). What I know for sure, since 2020 summer working-from-home situation, is that light mode is way better out on the balcony. I challenge you to read something in dark mode when enjoying the bright sunlight, sipping some cold beverage out on the balcony (if you're lucky to have one near).
